Louvre Abu Dhabi Signage + Pictogram Design

For my first work experience, I travelled to Paris to work with Clovis Vallois and Philippe Apeloig as an intern at Ateliers Jean Nouvel.

There I worked on the design of the signage and pictograms inspired by the structure of the light seeping through the dome structure of the museum.

I worked on the iterative design of the pictograms and mocked many physical props for the signage at real physical size.

The most challenging aspects of this projects were working with strict accessibility guidelines along with designing with three languages in mind (English, French and Arabic).
